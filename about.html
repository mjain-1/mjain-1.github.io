About

Megha Jain has an extensive background in environmental policy. Her previous role at the Urban Green Council working with the New York City Mayor's Office of Sustainability to analyze buildings' energy and water use led to an interest in data science.

To that end, she most recently completed a course in data science through Metis. She is interested in working with organizations to collect and analyze data in order to make better-informed policy or business decisions. You can find out more about her experience here.
