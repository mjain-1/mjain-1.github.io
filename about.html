About

Megha Jain has an extensive background in environmental policy. Her previous role at the Urban Green Council involved working with the New York City Mayor's Office of Sustainability to analyze buildings' energy and water use. Through that experience, she became interested in data science and thinking about how the data collection process and implicit biases can impact analyses.

In order to learn about the growing field of data science, she enrolled in a bootcamp through Metis. After accomplishing this course, she is interested in working with organizations to think through how to best use their data. You can find out more about her experience here.
