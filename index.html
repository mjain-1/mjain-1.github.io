Projects

    • Tracking Media Coverage on Climate Change

    • Tools

      scrapy, selenium, pandas, MIT NER, scikit-learn, flask, D3

    • This project sought to track media coverage on climate change since the 1990s focusing on regional reporting during weather events, specifically droughts, hurricanes, tornadoes, and flooding. Scraping articles from 12 different news sources, it aimed to answer whether these events are increasingly discussed in the context of climate change, as the science linking it to the frequency or severity of these events has become more certain, and whether there are any regional differences. The final product is a d3 interactive dashboard.
